Pebble Error Codes

Understanding and resolving common Pebble error codes

Error Codes FE504510, FE504511, FE504512

These error codes typically indicate a connection issue between your Pebble watch and your smartphone. Here's how to resolve them:

Troubleshooting Steps:
  1. Restart your Pebble watch (hold the back button for 10 seconds)
  2. Restart your smartphone
  3. Make sure Bluetooth is enabled on your phone
  4. Try forgetting the Pebble in your Bluetooth settings and reconnecting
  5. Reinstall the Pebble app
If you continue to experience these errors, you may need to factory reset your Pebble watch.
