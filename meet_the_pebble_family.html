Meet the Pebble Family

Explore the complete lineup of Pebble smartwatches

Original Pebble

The first Pebble smartwatch that started it all, launched via Kickstarter in 2013.

  • Released: 2013
  • Display: 1.26-inch e-paper
  • Battery: Up to 7 days
  • Water Resistance: 5 ATM

Pebble Steel

A premium version of the original Pebble with a stainless steel body.

  • Released: 2014
  • Display: 1.26-inch e-paper
  • Battery: Up to 7 days
  • Water Resistance: 5 ATM

Pebble Time

Introduced a color e-paper display and a new timeline interface.

  • Released: 2015
  • Display: 1.25-inch color e-paper
  • Battery: Up to 7 days
  • Water Resistance: 3 ATM

Pebble Time Steel

The premium version of the Pebble Time with a stainless steel body and longer battery life.

  • Released: 2015
  • Display: 1.25-inch color e-paper
  • Battery: Up to 10 days
  • Water Resistance: 3 ATM

Pebble Time Round

The thinnest and lightest smartwatch at the time, featuring a round display.

  • Released: 2015
  • Display: 1.0-inch color e-paper
  • Battery: Up to 2 days
  • Water Resistance: Splash resistant

Pebble 2

Added heart rate monitoring and a more fitness-focused design.

  • Released: 2016
  • Display: 1.26-inch e-paper
  • Battery: Up to 7 days
  • Water Resistance: 3 ATM
  • Features: Heart rate monitor
